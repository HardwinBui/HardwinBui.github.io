About Me

Hello! I'm Hardwin Bui, a recently graduated Computer Science major and I'm currently looking to help develop fun video games. During my free time, I often experiment with game design and work on small projects to better understand what could make for an entertaining experience. I've contributed to two college-student indie games and made a few small games of my own. My dream is to be able to help create something that people enjoy enough to the point where they want to share their experiences of it with others.
